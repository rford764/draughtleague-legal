Delete Your DraughtLeague Account

You can request deletion of your DraughtLeague account and all associated data at any time.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the DraughtLeague app on your device
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Sign out
  4. To request full account deletion, email us at privacy@draughtleague.com with the subject line "Delete my account" and include your registered email address

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile (username, email, date of birth, preferences)
  • Your check-in history and scores
  • Photos you uploaded
  • Your league memberships

What is retained

  • League leaderboard data may be retained in anonymised aggregate form
  • If you were a league commissioner, the league itself may remain but commissioner duties will be reassigned
⚠️ Account deletion is permanent. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Please make sure you want to permanently delete your account before requesting deletion.
Need help? Contact us at privacy@draughtleague.com and we will process your request within 30 days.
